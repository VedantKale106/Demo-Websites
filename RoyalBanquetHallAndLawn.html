About Royal Banquet Hall & Lawn

Royal Banquet Hall & Lawn in Thergaon, Pimpri-Chinchwad is a spacious and elegant event venue perfect for weddings, receptions, engagements, birthday parties, and corporate functions.

Our venue offers beautiful décor, professional service, spacious lawns, and a premium ambience that makes every celebration unforgettable. We pride ourselves on creating a regal atmosphere where every detail is managed with precision.
